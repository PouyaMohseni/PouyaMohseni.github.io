Handwritten Music Symbol Recognition

Optical music recognition (OMR) is a field of research that focuses on developing systems and algorithms capable of automatically interpreting and analyzing handwritten or printed music scores. In this reseach we explore relevent datasets and models for this task. Ultimetly, a multi-layer ensemble model, leveraged by the transfer learning approach is proposed, which was able to outcompete the 2022 SOTA by 0.5%.

Jazz Improvisation Generation

Jazz improvisation is the spontaneous creation of music, incorporating the artist's creativity and allowing for unique expressions. Generating jazz music using genetic algorithms is beneficial due to the inherent randomness and crossover of successful patterns. Surveying the previous studies, I proposed a novel evaluation function based on Graph Conditional Variational Autoencoders, incorporating a hierarchical representation of musical pieces.
