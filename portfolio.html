On Instrument and Dastgah Recognition in Persian Traditional Music

[Under Preparation for Submission]

Self-supervised learning has shown promise in extracting meaningful information from speech and music. However, its application in Persian Traditional Music remains underexplored. This research leverages self-supervised methods to address downstream tasks specifically tailored to this music domain, contributing to a deeper understanding and preservation of this culturally significant heritage.

Handwritten Music Symbol Recognition

Optical music recognition (OMR) focuses on proposing models that are capable of interpreting handwritten or printed music scores images. OMR plays a vital role in digitizing and processing musical compositions, ultimately contributing to music outreach and preserving heritage of the music. In this research, I propose different architectures employing a two-layer ensemble approach to achieve competitive results in this task compared to under-study paper (SOTA).

Jazz Improvisation Generation

Jazz improvisation is the spontaneous creation of music, incorporating the artist's creativity and allowing for unique expressions. Generating jazz music using genetic algorithms is beneficial due to the inherent randomness and crossover of successful patterns. Surveying the previous studies, I proposed a novel evaluation function based on Graph Conditional Variational Autoencoders, incorporating a hierarchical representation of musical pieces (only previously proposed approaches are used to generate the demo).

A Dataset for Multiple Instrument Recognition in Persian Traditional Music

[Early Stages]

Despite considerable advancements in polyphonic instrument recognition in Western music, this task is underexplored in Persian traditional music. In this project, we aim to address the gap by introducing a novel dataset specifically designed for multiple instrument recognition in Persian traditional music. This dataset comprises 5-second excerpts labeled for 10 instruments commonly used in Persian traditional music.
